Health Benefits of Table Tennis

Table tennis is more than just a game—it’s a way to boost your physical and mental health. Here are some key benefits:

  • Improves Reflexes: The fast-paced nature of table tennis sharpens reflexes and hand-eye coordination.
  • Enhances Mental Agility: Strategizing during play helps boost cognitive skills.
  • Low-Impact Exercise: It’s a great way to stay active without putting stress on joints.
  • Social Interaction: Playing with friends or joining a club fosters social connections.
