Equipment for Table Tennis

Whether you're setting up for professional play or just for fun, here's what you'll need to get started:

Official Equipment:

  • Table: A standard table tennis table is 9 feet long, 5 feet wide, and 30 inches high, with a net dividing it in the middle.
  • Racket: A high-quality paddle with a rubber surface for spin and control.
  • Balls: Official table tennis balls are 40mm in diameter and made of celluloid or plastic.
  • Net: A regulation net is 6 inches high and spans the width of the table.

Casual Setup:

  • Dining Table: You can use a flat dining table as a substitute for a regulation table.
  • Clip-On Net: Easily attachable nets can transform any table into a table tennis setup.
  • Basic Rackets and Balls: Affordable beginner kits are perfect for casual play.
